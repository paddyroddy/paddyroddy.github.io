Running

I began running in 2011 in training for a local 10km and quickly became hooked. I am a member of Thames Hare & Hounds and Kenilworth Runners, and previously competed for Cambridge University Hare & Hounds and UCL Athletics & Cross Country Club. Running has become a huge part of my life, and it is something I try to balance with working.
